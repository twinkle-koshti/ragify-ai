The RAGify Advantage

RAGify uses Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to turn your static PDF documents into a dynamic knowledge base. Instead of generic AI answers, RAGify retrieves specific facts from your uploaded files to ensure every response is accurate and cited.

Intelligent Processing Modes

Our model adapts to your needs. Simply upload a PDF and select your preferred output mode:

01

Smart Q&A

Ask anything about your document. Our model performs a semantic search to find the exact paragraph that answers your query.

02

MCQ Generator

Perfect for students. The model analyzes the PDF content to generate multiple-choice questions for self-testing and exam prep.

03

Executive Summary

Don't have time to read 50 pages? Select 'Summary' to get a concise, high-level overview of the most critical points in the document.

04

Flashcard Mode

Automatically convert complex concepts within your PDF into digitized flashcards for active recall and long-term memory.

How RAGify Works

AI

Vector Embedding

When you upload a PDF, we break it into "chunks" and convert them into numerical vectors that the AI can understand.

DB

PDF-Wise Chat Storage

Your workspace is organized. RAGify stores your chat history PDF-wise, meaning you can jump back into a conversation for a specific document without losing context.
